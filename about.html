About Us


Government Co-ed Senior Secondary School Site-2 Sector-6 Dwarka began in 2005. Today it is a co-educational school with classes VI to XII (Science Commerce and Arts, all three streams offered in +2) with Hindi and English medium. Affiliated to CBSE Board and having trained faculty and student strength of around 2000, the School is situated at Dwarka Sector-6 New Delhi. It is managed by a registered Educational Society.

The intellectual and moral development of the child to be a responsible global citizen and imparting learning which will help them to get a good livelihood are the core intentions of Government Co-ed Senior Secondary School Site-2 Sector-6 Dwarka. We follow an integrated curriculum in academics and sports to achieve this goal.
